About Me

Greetings, I am Dr. Alessandro De Luca, a dedicated neurosurgeon with a passion for saving lives and transforming healthcare in Africa. With an illustrious career spanning over two decades, I have revolutionized neurosurgical practices and implemented cutting-edge techniques in some of the most underserved regions of the world. My journey is fueled by a relentless pursuit of excellence and an unwavering commitment to making a difference.

I graduated with honors from the University of Florence, where I also completed my residency in neurosurgery. My postdoctoral training took me to Johns Hopkins University, where I specialized in minimally invasive neurosurgical techniques. Today, I split my time between Italy and Africa, working tirelessly to bring advanced medical care to those who need it most.

My Work

My expertise encompasses a wide array of neurosurgical procedures, from pioneering minimally invasive brain surgeries to leading complex spinal operations. I have partnered with top-tier hospitals and non-profit organizations to enhance healthcare infrastructure, ensuring that even the remotest areas have access to world-class medical care. Through my work, I aim to leave an indelible mark on the global medical community.

Some of the key areas I specialize in include:

  • Brain Tumor Removal
  • Spinal Cord Injuries
  • Neurovascular Surgery
  • Pediatric Neurosurgery
  • Epilepsy Surgery

Projects

My groundbreaking projects include:

  • Kenya Neurosurgical Training Program - Empowering local surgeons with advanced skills and knowledge.
  • Uganda Brain Tumor Initiative - Introducing state-of-the-art brain tumor treatment techniques.
  • Tanzania Spinal Rehabilitation Project - Establishing comprehensive spinal injury care and rehabilitation centers.
  • Sierra Leone Mobile Neurosurgery Units - Deploying mobile units to deliver critical care to remote villages.
  • Nigeria Epilepsy Surgery Initiative - Providing surgical solutions for epilepsy in regions lacking specialized care.
