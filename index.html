About Me

Hello, I'm Dr. Lorenzo von Matterhorn, a dedicated neurosurgeon with a passion for saving lives in Africa. With over a decade of experience, I have worked tirelessly to bring advanced neurosurgical techniques to underserved communities. My mission is to provide the highest quality of care to my patients and to train local medical professionals, ensuring sustainable healthcare development.

My Work

My work spans various aspects of neurosurgery, from performing complex brain surgeries to treating spinal disorders. I have collaborated with numerous hospitals and non-profit organizations to improve healthcare infrastructure and accessibility in remote areas. Through my work, I strive to make a significant impact on the lives of my patients and the medical community at large.

Projects

Some of my notable projects include:

  • Neurosurgical Training Program in Kenya
  • Advanced Brain Tumor Surgery Initiative in Uganda
  • Spinal Injury Rehabilitation Project in Tanzania
